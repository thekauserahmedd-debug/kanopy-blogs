What is KWIN City?

Karnataka's Most Ambitious Smart City Project

KWIN stands for Knowledge, Wellbeing and Innovation — a 5,800-acre planned city being developed between Doddaballapur and Dabaspet, 45 minutes from Kempegowda International Airport, Bangalore.

Four Integrated Districts
Knowledge, Health, Innovation and Research — designed to work as a single integrated ecosystem, not isolated zones.
15-Minute City Design
Grid-system layout ensuring residents can travel from any point to another within 15 minutes. No congestion by design.
40% Green Cover
40% of KWIN City dedicated to parks, open spaces and over 10 lakes connected by canal systems throughout the city.

"KWIN City represents Karnataka's bold vision for the future — a state-of-the-art hub for knowledge, health, innovation, and research. We are creating an ecosystem that will drive economic growth, attract global talent, and foster groundbreaking advancements."

Shri Siddaramaiah — Hon'ble Chief Minister of Karnataka

Smart & Seamless Connectivity

Infrastructure That Already Exists — and What's Coming

The Satellite Town Ring Road (STRR) is operational. This is not a future promise — physical connectivity to Hebbal, Yelahanka, and the airport road exists today. KWIN City sits directly on this corridor.

  • Monorail — Central rapid transit with stops every 800 metres within KWIN City
  • STRR — Already operational, connecting Nelamangala to Bangalore North
  • National Highways — NH 44 and NH 648 direct access
  • Airport — 45 minutes to Kempegowda International Airport
  • Express Rail — Adjacent to Bangalore–Hubballi–Mumbai Express rail route
  • Metro Extension — Tumakuru road metro extension under consideration
  • Bangalore-Pune Greenfield Expressway — Direct connection planned
  • Bike-sharing + EV transport — Eco-friendly intra-city mobility systems

KWIN City — Frequently Asked Questions

What does KWIN City stand for?
KWIN stands for Knowledge, Wellbeing and Innovation City. It was previously called KHIR City (Knowledge, Health, Innovation and Research) before being officially renamed KWIN to better reflect its focus on wellbeing alongside innovation. It was officially launched on September 26, 2024 by Karnataka Chief Minister Siddaramaiah.
How many acres is KWIN City and where is it located?
KWIN City spans 5,800 acres in total, located between Doddaballapur and Dabaspet in Bengaluru Rural district, Karnataka. It is approximately 45 minutes from Kempegowda International Airport and 50 km from Bangalore city centre. It sits directly on the Satellite Town Ring Road (STRR) corridor in North Bangalore.
How many residents will KWIN City accommodate?
KWIN City is designed to accommodate approximately 5,00,000 (five lakh) residents at full buildout. The city is master-planned as a complete urban ecosystem with housing, jobs, parks, roads and social infrastructure for its entire population.
Does KWIN City have a monorail?
Yes. KWIN City's master plan includes a central monorail system providing rapid transit within the city, with stops every 800 metres. This is complemented by a grid-road network of arterial, sub-arterial, distributor and local roads, bike-sharing systems and other eco-friendly transport options. The design ensures residents can travel between any two points in the city within 15 minutes.
Which universities and institutions are planned for KWIN City?
KWIN City aims to attract the top 500 foreign educational institutions to operate campuses in India under UGC regulations. Universities in discussions or confirmed include St. John's University (New York), University of York (UK), Azim Premji University, PES University, Manipal Academy, IISc, NIMHANS, JNCASR, and Tata IISc Medical School. Major MoUs were signed at Invest Karnataka 2025 with top international universities from USA, UK and Canada.
How many jobs will KWIN City create?
KWIN City is projected to create approximately 80,000 to 1,00,000 direct employment opportunities in research, health-tech, biotech, skill development, education and innovation sectors. Indirect employment from hospitality, retail, logistics, and supporting businesses will add significantly to this number.
What is KWIN City's total investment target?
KWIN City is expected to attract ₹40,000 crore in domestic and international investment across education, healthcare, innovation and research sectors. The Government of Karnataka is providing land equity while the private sector drives investment into institutional, commercial and residential development.
