What Makes This Corridor Different

Most real estate corridors in Bangalore get talked about for years before anything happens. The Nelamangala–Doddaballapur belt is different. The KWIN City project — Karnataka's ₹40,000 crore Knowledge, Wellbeing and Innovation city — was not a press release. It was launched on September 26, 2024 by Chief Minister Siddaramaiah, with Kiran Mazumdar-Shaw and Dr. Devi Shetty on the stage as institutional backers.

The STRR (Satellite Town Ring Road) is already operational and physically connecting Nelamangala to the rest of the city. Phase 1 infrastructure across 500 acres of KWIN City is active. These are not announcements. They are ground facts.

Common Questions About Buying Plots Near KWIN City

What is the current land price near KWIN City Nelamangala?
Prices vary by proximity to KWIN City, road access, and BMRDA approval status. BMRDA-approved gated plots are trading in the ₹2,000–₹4,500 per sq ft range as of early 2026 depending on the specific layout. Contact Kanopy Ventures for current inventory pricing.
Is KWIN City confirmed or just a proposal?
KWIN City was officially launched on September 26, 2024 by Karnataka Chief Minister Siddaramaiah. Phase 1 infrastructure across 500 acres is active. It is a confirmed, funded, and operational project — not a proposal.
Can NRIs buy plots near KWIN City?
Yes. NRIs from USA, UK, Canada, Singapore, UAE, Australia, Germany and Netherlands can legally purchase BMRDA-approved residential plots in Karnataka. Kanopy Ventures handles 100% of the process remotely including power of attorney, legal verification, and registration support.
How far is the Nelamangala corridor from Bangalore city?
The Nelamangala–Doddaballapur corridor is 35–50 km from central Bangalore depending on the specific location. With the STRR operational, connectivity to Hebbal, Yelahanka, and the airport road is significantly improved.
